Next level K-5 education begins here

Teaching will never be the same. Our platform offers adaptive learning, class management, course design, and just about anything you can think of.

No student, teacher, or parent left behind.

Students

Struggling in school can feel like you fell off a train and you're now lost in the woods. Instead of letting students fall behind further and further, we automatically deliver material that focuses on each student's unique foundational misunderstandings. By doing this, every student has the ability to reach their fullest potential.

Teachers

A virtual classroom that feels as authentic as the real one. Here you can host your own personal courses, lessons, and practice or even use content from another teacher. We provide performance results for each student that diagnoses weak points in their understanding, so you can refine your instruction to accommodate for that.

Parents

For centuries, parents had to pry out what their child learned in class with the classic "How was school today?" Parents now have transparent access to see exactly what their student is working on and how well they are getting the material. On top of that, we also provide robust analytics to show your child's progress over time.
